Recognizing Similar Relationships Within Ontology to Fine Tune Ontology

Authors: Neelam Chandolikar, Rishav Raj, Rohit Mujumdar

Keywords: knowledge graphs, natural language processing, semantic search engine, ontology learning, triplet extraction, education technology

Conference: ICDMAI

Summary: Ontology learning process involves identification of concepts and the relationships between these concepts. Automated ontology learning based on ML/DL techniques identifies these triples but suffers from the problem of duplicate or similar relationships. We propose a solution to identify similar relationships so that the ontology can be fine-tuned.
